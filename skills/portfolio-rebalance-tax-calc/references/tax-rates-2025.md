## Key Tax Rules

### Wash Sale Rule
- Cannot deduct loss if you buy "substantially identical" security within 30 days before or after the sale
- Applies across all accounts (taxable, IRA, spouse's accounts)
- Disallowed loss is added to the cost basis of the replacement shares

### Capital Loss Limitations
- Net capital losses can offset up to $3,000 of ordinary income per year ($1,500 MFS)
- Excess losses carry forward indefinitely
- Short-term losses first offset short-term gains, then long-term gains
- Long-term losses first offset long-term gains, then short-term gains

### Holding Period
- Short-term: held 1 year or less → taxed as ordinary income
- Long-term: held more than 1 year → preferential rates (0%/15%/20%)

### Qualified Dividends Requirements
- Held stock for 60+ days during 121-day period around ex-dividend date
- From US corporation or qualified foreign corporation
- Not from tax-exempt organization or farmer's cooperative

### Standard Deduction (2025)
- Single: $15,000
- MFJ: $30,000
- HoH: $22,500
